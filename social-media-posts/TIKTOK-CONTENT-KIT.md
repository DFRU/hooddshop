## STEP 9 — Filming Tips

1. **Vertical only** — 9:16 aspect ratio, always
2. **Natural lighting** — film outside or near windows
3. **Hook in first 2 seconds** — text overlay or dramatic action immediately
4. **No long intros** — skip "hey guys" openings. Jump straight to the content.
5. **Use trending sounds** — check TikTok's Discover page daily
6. **Add text overlays** — many viewers watch without sound
7. **Keep it 15-30 seconds** for most content (longer for BTS/storytelling)
8. **Film in batches** — spend 1 hour filming 5-10 videos, then post over the week
