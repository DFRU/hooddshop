## STEP 2 — Cross-Posting Strategy

YouTube Shorts = same vertical videos you film for TikTok. The workflow:

1. Film video on your phone (9:16, under 60 seconds)
2. Post to TikTok first
3. Remove TikTok watermark (use SnapTik or SaveTik app, or re-export from your camera roll)
4. Upload to YouTube Shorts
5. Upload to Instagram Reels

**Critical rule:** YouTube's algorithm deprioritizes videos with TikTok watermarks. Always upload the clean, non-watermarked version.

## STEP 4 — Shorts-Specific Video Ideas

All TikTok videos from TIKTOK-CONTENT-KIT.md work on Shorts. These additional formats perform well specifically on YouTube:

### "How To" Format (YouTube loves tutorials)
- Title: "How to Install a World Cup Hood Cover in 10 Seconds"
- Show step by step: unfold, stretch over hood, tuck edges, done
- Text overlays for each step

### "Before & After" Format
- Title: "Car Transformation: World Cup 2026 Edition"
- Show plain car > cut > car with hood cover
- Dramatic reveal with music

### "Top 5" / "Ranking" Format
- Title: "Top 5 World Cup 2026 Hood Cover Designs"
- Quick slideshow ranking 5 nations
- End with "Which is #1? Comment below"

### "Reply to Comments" Format
- Title: "Replying to @username — Yes, we have [Country]!"
- Great for engagement loops once you start getting comments
