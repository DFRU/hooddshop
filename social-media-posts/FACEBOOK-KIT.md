## STEP 5 — Facebook Groups Strategy (Highest ROI)

This is the most powerful free marketing channel for Hood'd Shop. Join and post in these group types:

### Groups to Find and Join

Search Facebook for these terms and join the largest/most active groups:

**By nation (search "[Country] fans in USA" or "[Country] community [City]"):**
- "Mexican soccer fans USA"
- "Argentina futbol fans"
- "Brazil fans in America"
- "Colombia soccer fans"
- "Nigeria fans USA"
- "England football fans USA"
- "Germany soccer fans America"

**By host city (search "World Cup 2026 [City]"):**
- "World Cup 2026 Miami"
- "World Cup 2026 Dallas"
- "World Cup 2026 Houston"
- "World Cup 2026 New York"
- "World Cup 2026 Los Angeles"
- "World Cup 2026 Toronto"

**By interest:**
- "Car wraps" / "Car culture" groups
- "Soccer fans USA"
- "World Cup 2026 fans"
- "Car mods and accessories"

### Group Posting Rules

1. **Read each group's rules before posting** — many ban direct sales posts
2. **Lead with value, not sales** — "Check out what I made for World Cup" not "BUY NOW"
3. **Post as a fan, not a brand** — "Fellow [Country] fans, made these hood covers for our cars"
4. **Include a photo** — always. Posts with images get 2-3x the engagement.
5. **Limit to 2-3 group posts per day** to avoid being flagged as spam
6. **Engage in comments** — answer questions, thank people, build community

### Group Post Template
```
Fellow [COUNTRY] fans! Made these custom car hood covers for World Cup 2026. Stretch fabric, slides right over the hood, off in seconds. Perfect for match day.

48 nations available. $44.99 with free shipping.

Anyone else repping their country on their car this summer?

hooddshop.com (code HOODDSHIP for free shipping)
```
