## Document Purpose

A one-page collaboration agreement between **Hood'd Shop Inc.** ("Hood'd") and **[Creator Legal Name]** ("Creator") for a single limited-edition product drop. Designed to be signable within 48 hours of first contact. Not a master services agreement. Each drop is its own agreement.

---

## 1. The Collaboration

Creator and Hood'd will collaborate on the design of a single limited-edition car hood cover (the "Drop Product") under the Hood'd Shop brand. The Drop Product will be sold exclusively on hooddshop.com for a time-limited window defined in Section 4.

**Drop Slug:** `[e.g., creator-handle-nation-date]`
**Drop Nation / Theme:** `[e.g., Mexico — Día de Muertos edition]`
**Retail Price:** `$[64.99 or 69.99] USD`

## 2. Creator Deliverables

Creator agrees to provide:

1. Initial design input via one design kickoff session (async or live, approximately 30 minutes)
2. Written approval of final design (email or messaging platform acceptable)
3. Minimum promotional content on Creator's owned channels (Instagram, TikTok, YouTube, and/or X as applicable):
   - **1 teaser post** 3–5 days before drop launch
   - **1 launch post** within 2 hours of drop going live
   - **1 recap post** within 48 hours of drop close
   - Minimum **2 Stories** across the drop window
4. Confidentiality regarding the design, pricing, and launch date until drop goes live.

## 3. Hood'd Deliverables

Hood'd agrees to provide:

1. Design production — up to 3 rounds of refinement based on Creator input
2. Print-ready files delivered to manufacturing partner
3. Product page build-out, countdown mechanic, and drop infrastructure
4. Fulfillment from order placement through customer delivery
5. Customer service for all orders
6. Two complimentary units of the Drop Product shipped to Creator at Hood'd's cost
7. Post-drop settlement statement and payment per Section 5

## 4. Drop Window

- **Drop opens:** `[Date, 10:00am ET]`
- **Drop closes:** 48 hours after opening
- **Post-drop availability:** None. Product is permanently unavailable after window close.

Hood'd reserves the right to adjust opening time by up to 24 hours with 48 hours advance notice to Creator.

## 5. Revenue Share

Creator shall receive **$7.00 USD per unit sold** during the drop window.

- Payment calculated based on Shopify's reported completed-order count, excluding refunded or chargeback orders
- Payment issued within 14 calendar days of drop close
- Payment method: `[Stripe Connect / PayPal / Wire / Check — to be agreed]`
- Creator is responsible for all applicable taxes on revenue received
- Creator agrees to provide tax documentation as required by applicable law (W-8BEN / W-9 / equivalent) before first payment

## 6. Intellectual Property

1. **Design IP.** All final design elements, including variations developed during the iteration process, are the exclusive property of Hood'd. Creator retains no rights to the final design or to manufacture, distribute, or license the design outside this agreement.
2. **Creator Contributions.** Creator hereby assigns any rights in design inputs, sketches, or references provided to Hood'd to Hood'd in perpetuity. Creator represents that no assigned materials infringe the rights of any third party.
3. **Content.** Creator retains ownership of promotional content they produce. Creator grants Hood'd a perpetual, royalty-free, worldwide license to repost, reuse, and remix that content in Hood'd marketing materials.
4. **Hood'd brand.** Creator receives no rights to the Hood'd trademarks, brand assets, or any other Hood'd IP beyond what is necessary to execute this agreement.

## 7. Representations and Restrictions

Creator represents that:

1. They have not granted exclusive representation rights to any agent, manager, or platform that would conflict with this agreement.
2. They are at least 18 years of age or have a legal guardian co-signing this agreement.
3. No aspect of the collaboration will include or reference any third party's trademark, likeness, copyrighted work, or protected IP without that party's written permission.

Creator agrees NOT to:

1. Publicly disclose the Drop Product design, pricing, or launch date before drop launch
2. Sell, transfer, or license the Drop Product design to any third party
3. Produce or sell any product substantially similar to the Drop Product for a period of 90 days after drop close
4. Make claims about Hood'd, its products, or its business that are inaccurate or disparaging

## 8. Kill Clause

Either party may terminate this agreement before design finalization:

- **Hood'd may terminate** if Creator fails to respond to design iteration communication for more than 7 consecutive days, or materially fails to meet the delivery schedule. Upon termination Hood'd may complete the design independently and proceed without Creator; Creator forfeits any rev share.
- **Creator may terminate** before final design approval with written notice; no fault or penalty.
- After final design approval, neither party may withdraw.

## 9. Confidentiality

Both parties agree to hold confidential information received from the other party in confidence, including design iterations, sales figures, and business strategy. Obligation survives 12 months after drop close.

## 10. Liability

Neither party's liability to the other shall exceed the total revenue share paid or payable under this agreement. Neither party is liable for indirect, consequential, or punitive damages. Standard limitations and exclusions apply.

## 11. Governing Law

This agreement is governed by the laws of `[Ontario, Canada or applicable province/state]`. Disputes shall be resolved first through good-faith negotiation, then binding arbitration in `[city]` if unresolved.

## 12. Entire Agreement

This document constitutes the entire agreement between the parties with respect to this Drop Product and supersedes any prior discussions. Modifications must be in writing and signed by both parties.
